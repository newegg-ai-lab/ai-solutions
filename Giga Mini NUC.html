01 What you get

Everything for a private assistant, on the box.

No accounts to create, no keys to manage, no usage meter. You pull a model once and it runs locally from then on.

CHAT

Local chat assistant

Talk to a 7B model running on the machine. Answers stream into your browser — none of it is sent to a server.

UI

Built-in web interface

A clean chat page at localhost:8080 with a model picker and a live engine selector.

GPU

GPU-accelerated

The Arc integrated GPU runs your everyday model over Vulkan, keeping all 14 CPU cores free for everything else.

NPU

An efficiency lane

A second, sip-power engine runs smaller models on the NPU through OpenVINO — built for always-on, background work.

PRIV

Fully private

Every model and conversation stays on the machine. It works with the network unplugged.

SCALE

Room to grow

28 GB of shared memory fits models up to ~14B. Add a new one with a single command.

02 Where it fits

Built for small teams that want AI without the cloud bill.

Three engines handle different jobs at the same time — nothing waits in line.

CPU · Runs the show — GPU · Does the heavy lifting — NPU · Always on, barely sips power

Text Q&A
Ask questions about anything you paste in
Paste in a policy, contract clause, or email thread and ask questions about it in plain English — the model reasons over whatever you give it, right in the chat. For searching across your whole document library automatically, that's what the RTX Hub's document search is built for.
Help Desk
An internal assistant that's always on
Answer HR, IT, and operations questions around the clock. Staff get answers in seconds without pulling someone away from their work.
Finance & Admin
Draft & review financial write-ups
Paste in numbers, a draft memo, or a report and get help summarizing it, tightening the language, or sanity-checking the math — right in the chat window, nothing leaves the box. Good for everyday admin writing, not a substitute for an automated invoice-processing pipeline.
Development
Private code assistant
Completions, reviews, and explanations from a model running entirely on the box. Your codebase never touches an external server.
Meetings
Meeting notes from your transcript
Paste in a transcript from your call (Zoom, Teams, wherever it was recorded) and get a clean summary with action items in seconds — processed entirely on the box, never sent to a summarization API.
Personal Workstation
Your own always-on AI station
A private, always-on assistant for one desk — no per-seat subscriptions, no rack, no IT overhead, just a quiet box that's always ready when you are.
