01Real-World Use Cases

Six ways Hailo Hub earns its place.

On-device vision, speech, and chat solving real operational problems — no cloud required.

📦
Automated Returns Pre-Sort
Every returned item gets scanned the moment it arrives — visible damage flagged, RMA number read straight off the label, item pre-sorted into the right bin. A person still makes the final call, but nobody has to eyeball every box first.
🚚
Package Integrity Gate
Every outbound box passes a camera before leaving the dock. Crushed corners, torn tape, and compromised packaging are flagged automatically — stopping damaged shipments before they reach the customer.
🦺
PPE & Safety Monitoring
Automatically detect whether workers in restricted zones are wearing required safety gear. Get real-time alerts without a supervisor needing to physically walk the floor.
🎙️
Meeting Transcription & Translation
Record a meeting or vendor call and get a clean, timestamped transcript — entirely offline, nothing leaves the building. Non-English speech gets translated to English automatically, so mixed-language calls are easy to review later.
🔍
Visual Product Q&A
Snap a photo of a product, a damaged item, or a shelf and get an instant first read in plain language — what it is, what shape it's in, what the label says. Good for a quick check before looping in a specialist for anything tricky.
✍️
Private Writing & Drafting Assistant
A private chat assistant for drafting emails, summarizing text, or thinking through a quick problem — fully on-device, nothing sent to an outside AI service. For deep Q&A over your actual policy library, that's what the RTX Hub's document search is built for.
